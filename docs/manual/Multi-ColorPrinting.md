The Creator 5 series supports multi-color G-code and 3mf files.

For G-code files, the printer automatically maps extruders 1–4 to the corresponding filaments defined in the slice. This assignment is fixed and cannot be changed on the printer.

For 3mf files, you can manually assign extruders on the screen. Selecting two or more extruders enables multi-color printing.
