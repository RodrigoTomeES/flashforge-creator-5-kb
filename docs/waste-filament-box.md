**Step files:**

[Poop Bin (Hanger Version).step](https://wiki.flashforge.com/resource/pictures/%E4%B8%AD%E6%96%87/creator5/other/%E6%94%B6%E6%96%99%E7%9B%92\(%E6%8C%82%E6%9E%B6%E7%89%88\).step)

[Hanger + Poop Bin\_PLA\_2h51m.gcode](https://wiki.flashforge.com/resource/pictures/%E4%B8%AD%E6%96%87/creator5/other/%E6%8C%82%E6%9E%B6%E6%94%B6%E6%96%99%E7%9B%92_pla_2h51m.gcode)

**2.Print the downloaded file(s) using your 3D printer.**

**3\. Installation**  
Step 1: Apply double-sided adhesive tape to the contact surface between the hanger and the machine, then secure it to the rear right side of the machine as shown below：

![图片2e.png](https://wiki.flashforge.com/resource/pictures/%E4%B8%AD%E6%96%87/creator5/other/%E5%9B%BE%E7%89%872e.png)

Step 2: Attach the poop bin onto the hanger as shown below.

![图片3.png](https://wiki.flashforge.com/resource/pictures/%E4%B8%AD%E6%96%87/creator5/other/%E5%9B%BE%E7%89%873.png)

**4\. Important Notes：**  
During the filament purging process, purged filament may occasionally stick to the side wall, causing the waste to pile up, as shown below:

![图片4.png](https://wiki.flashforge.com/resource/pictures/%E4%B8%AD%E6%96%87/creator5/other/%E5%9B%BE%E7%89%874.png)

If the pile builds up higher than the top edge of the poop bin, simply push the waste toward the side as shown below. The poop bin can then continue to be used normally.

![图片5.png](https://wiki.flashforge.com/resource/pictures/%E4%B8%AD%E6%96%87/creator5/other/%E5%9B%BE%E7%89%875.png)

> **Recommendation**: To prevent build-up, we recommend emptying the poop bin every time you remove a finished print, right before starting your next print job.
