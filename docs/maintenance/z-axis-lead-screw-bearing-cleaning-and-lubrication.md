> During long-term operation, the Z-axis bearing may accumulate some dust due to friction and the intrusion of external dust, which can cause jams and hinder smooth operation during platform operation. Minor issues may affect the appearance of the printed model, while severe cases can lead to laggy operation and abnormal platform behavior.

**1、Maintenance Cycle**

After the printer is activated, it is recommended to perform a cleaning, lubrication, and maintenance once every 3 months;

**2、Maintenance Tools**

WD-40 High-Efficiency Silicone Lubricant

Grease

Cleanroom paper/Cleanroom cloth
