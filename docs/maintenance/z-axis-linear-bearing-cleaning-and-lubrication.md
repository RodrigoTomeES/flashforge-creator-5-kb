> During long-term operation, the optical axis of the Z-axis may experience some particle crystallization on the guide rail due to friction and the intrusion of external dust. This can cause the Z-axis to run smoothly and become laggy during the operation of the print head. Slight issues may lead to the appearance or exacerbation of Z-direction layer lines in the printed model, while severe cases can cause lag and abnormal platform operation.

**1、Maintenance Cycle**

After the printer is activated, it is recommended to perform a cleaning, lubrication, and maintenance once every 3 months;

**2、Maintenance Tools**

WD-40 High-Efficiency Silicone Lubricant

Cleanroom Paper/Cleanroom Cloth
