> During long-term operation, the Z-axis belt may loosen due to environmental factors, wear, and loosening of mechanical components. A loose belt leads to reduced transmission accuracy and step loss during operation. In more severe cases, it may cause transmission interruption. Therefore, it is particularly important to regularly check the tightness of the belt and adjust the tension in a timely manner.

**1、Maintenance Frequency**

It is recommended to perform maintenance once every 3000 hours of printing;

**2、Maintenance Tools**

2.0mm and 2.5mm hex wrenches;

Timing belt tension testing tool (can be self-made by downloading the document);
