> During long-term operation, the Z-axis pulley may have some particle crystallization on its surface due to friction and the incorporation of external dust, which will cause the belt to jump during the platform operation. This will affect the appearance and dimensional quality of the printed model.

**1、Maintenance Cycle**

It is recommended to perform a cleaning, lubrication, and maintenance every 3000 hours of printing;

**2、Maintenance Tools**

WD-40 High-Efficiency Silicone Lubricant

Cleanroom paper/Cleanroom cloth
