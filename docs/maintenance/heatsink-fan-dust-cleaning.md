> During long-term use, dust will gradually accumulate on the blades of the cooling fan, and as time goes by, the dust will pile up thicker and thicker. This will cause the air volume of the fan to decrease, leading to a decline in the cooling function, and easily causing the temperature of the throat tube to rise, which increases the risk of blockage.

**1、Maintenance Cycle**

If the average daily working hours T < 5 hours, it is recommended to clean the equipment once every 9 months after activation;

If the average daily working hours are 5 hours < T < 10 hours, it is recommended to clean the equipment once every 6 months after activation;

If the average daily working hours T > 10 hours, it is recommended to clean the equipment once every 3 months after activation;

**2、Maintenance Tools**

Air Gun/Brush
