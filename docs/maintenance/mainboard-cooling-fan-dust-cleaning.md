> During long-term use of the motherboard cooling fan, dust will gradually accumulate on the fan blades, and as time passes, the dust will become thicker and thicker. This will cause the fan's air volume to decrease, leading to insufficient heat dissipation of the chip, which may cause the host computer to freeze or other abnormalities to occur.

**1、Maintenance Cycle**

-   If the average daily working hours T < 5 hours, it is recommended to clean the equipment once every 9 months after activation;
    
-   If the average daily working hours are 5 hours < T < 10 hours, it is recommended to clean the equipment once every 6 months after activation;
    
-   If the average daily working hours T > 10 hours, it is recommended to clean the equipment once every 3 months after activation；
    

**2、Maintenance Tools**

Air Gun/Brush
