> During long-term operation of the Z-axis lead screw, due to friction and the incorporation of external dust, some particles will accumulate in the grooves of the lead screw, which will cause the platform to run smoothly and become stuck during operation. Slight cases will affect the appearance of Z-axis lines on the printed model, and severe cases will cause laggy operation and lead to abnormal platform operation.

**1、Maintenance Cycle**

After the printer is activated, it is recommended to perform a cleaning, lubrication, and maintenance once every 3 months;

Grease

Cleanroom Paper/Cleanroom Cloth
