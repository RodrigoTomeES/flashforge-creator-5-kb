> During long-term operation of the Z-axis lead screw, due to friction and the incorporation of external dust, some particles will accumulate in the grooves of the lead screw, which will cause the platform to run smoothly and become stuck during operation. Slight cases will affect the appearance of Z-axis lines on the printed model, and severe cases will cause laggy operation and lead to abnormal platform operation.

**1、Maintenance Cycle**

Depending on the usage frequency, we recommend the following maintenance intervals:

High-frequency usage (average daily printing time ≥ 5 hours): perform deep maintenance of the Z axis once every 3 months.  
Regular usage (average daily printing time 1–5 hours):perform maintenance of the Z axis once every 4 months.  
Low-frequency usage (average daily printing time < 1 hour): perform maintenance of the Z axis once every 5 months.

**2、Maintenance Tools**
