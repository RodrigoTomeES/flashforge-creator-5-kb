> During long-term operation, the XY-axis pulley may have some particle crystallization on its surface due to friction and the incorporation of external dust, which will cause the belt to jump during the operation of the print head. This will affect the appearance and dimensional quality of the printed model.

**1、Maintenance Cycle**

t is recommended to perform a cleaning, lubrication, and maintenance once every 1500 hours of printing;

**2、Maintenance Tools**

WD-40 High-Efficiency Silicone Lubricant

Cleanroom paper/Cleanroom cloth
